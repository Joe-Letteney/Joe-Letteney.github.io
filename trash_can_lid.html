Trash Can Lid - Four Bar Linkage Design

This project focused on designing and simulating a four-bar linkage mechanism for a trash can lid. The goal was to create a smooth, efficient, and ergonomic opening and closing motion.

Design Details

  • Used SolidWorks Motion Analysis to optimize the linkage movement.
  • Designed for minimal effort when opening and closing the lid.
  • Ensured manufacturability with standard hinge and rod components.
