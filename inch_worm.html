Inch Worm Robot

Fabrication • Soft Robotics

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to desing a robot made of compliant (soft) materials that was capable of movement and grasping as well as incorperating some unique feature. For this project my team opted to go with an inchworm inspired robot that consisted if an accordian motor between two angular pouch motors that deploy silicone feet.

Step 1: Prototype 1

The first prototype of this robot consisted of an accordian motor with two inline pouch motors that deploy and retract silicone feet. One of the moajor issues with this prototype is that the inline pouch motors were not able to apply any force to the the feet so the amount of friciton produced was minimal.

Step 2: Protoype 2

In order to solve the problems that we encountered in the first protoype our second prototype included an angular pouch motor that consisted of an angled PLA strucutre that holds the pouch motor allowing it to apply force to the silicone foot.

Step 3: Final Design

Our final design included our steering elements, our grasper, and our angular pouch motors. This allowed us to incorperae our passive steering features as it ran into walls.
